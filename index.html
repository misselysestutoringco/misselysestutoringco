My Approach

How I Teach

Every student is different — so every session is different. Here's what you can always expect when working with Miss Elyse:

01

I Teach You, We Work Together, You Try Yourself!

For most concepts, I walk through an example first, then we work through one together, and then you try on your own — with support always available. It builds independence without overwhelm.

02

Guided Discovery Over Just Giving Answers

When a student is stuck, I ask the right questions to guide them toward the answer themselves. This builds true understanding and critical thinking rather than dependency.

03

Engaging & Age-Appropriate Learning

For younger students, sessions are filled with learning games, activities, and hands-on worksheets that make concepts click — because when kids are having fun, they're learning better.

04

Encouragement-First Confidence Building

I meet every student where they are and celebrate every step forward. Building genuine academic confidence is just as important as understanding the material.
